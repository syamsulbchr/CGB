About Us

Cogent Bladesmith Indonesia

  • Our Story

    Cogent Bladesmith are the leading and one of the best knives company in Indonesia, our company established in 2007. We produce a range of products of high quality handmade knives, with equaling quality with the world’s knife company. we are made with selected of varied materials, an artistic design, and customized to customer’s preference

  • Raw Material

    Our Product are use D2 tool steel witch is featured with high carbon content and high chromium, high hardness max 64 Hrc. Although fairly resistant but still not free of rust, but this material is very popular with users because of the high hardness knife so sharp durable and easily sharpened when blunt. Chemical composition is: C = 1.55%; Si = 0:25%; Mn = 0:35%; Cr = 11.8%; Mo = 0.80%; V = 0.95%
